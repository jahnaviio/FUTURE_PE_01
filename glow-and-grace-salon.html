✦ Our Story

Born out of a love for making women feel seen

Glow & Grace began with a simple belief — that luxury shouldn't be a luxury. Nestled in the heart of Visakhapatnam, our salon was built for women who deserve the very best without the eye-watering price tags.

From your first consultation to the final look in the mirror, every moment here is designed to make you feel pampered, valued, and completely at ease. Our team of certified stylists and beauty experts bring creativity, skill, and genuine warmth to everything they do.

Whether you're prepping for a big day, refreshing your everyday look, or simply treating yourself — this is your place to glow.
